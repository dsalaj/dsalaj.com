After your machine/device successfully finished the DNS lookup for this domain, and your browser managed to open a TCP connection to my server, you sent a HTTP request on which my server politely replied. Because the message was ugly, your browser rendered it, and we went through all this trouble so that only you could read this pointless message from the far edge of the nets.

It might be dark here, but that's because we learned how to conserve our energy. These packets you are assembling are served by a BeagleBone Black system consuming less then 2W of electricity! It is powered by Arch linux and nginx. In the future it might be powered by stored solar power or peltier elements, depending on the season. If you are following the pattern, you could conclude that I am interested in efficiency and follow "use only what you need" minimalism principle.

And by the way, I am ~dsalaj, the ruler of this domain (at least for now) and the operator of this tiny server. In the quantum analog world I am a student of Computer Science at TUG. You can read more about me here. Occasionally I have a habit of making noises. You can contact me via email.
